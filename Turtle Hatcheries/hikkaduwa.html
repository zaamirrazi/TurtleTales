Hikkaduwa

Sea Turtle Hatchery & Rescue Center, a modest yet lovely turtle farm and rescue facility, is located in Hikkaduwa. Visitors may view turtle eggs that are securely buried in the hatchery's sand here. Eggs typically hatch after two days. To ensure that they develop properly and may subsequently be returned into the water safely, where they can live in their native habitat, turtles are handled very delicately. Since turtles must develop in order to be released and continue living on their own, visitors may witness turtles of many different sizes here.
