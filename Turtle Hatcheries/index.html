Turtle Hatcheries in Sri Lanka

You Can Save More

A few kilometers north of Hikkaduwa town lies the Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center. It began as a neighborhood initiative to maintain biodiversity, but it has now developed into a full-fledged hatchery and rescue facility with a dedicated team that studies, looks after, and defends turtle species.
